## Keeping references current

Aviation regulations are updated regularly. The following practices will help you stay current:

- Subscribe to CASA's email update service at [casa.gov.au](https://www.casa.gov.au)
- Check NAIPS for NOTAM updates before every shift
- Review AIP amendments when published (typically every 28 days on the AIRAC cycle)
- Review the aerodrome manual annually or when notified of an amendment

> ⚠️ **Important:** Regulations change. This handbook is a learning resource, not the regulation itself. Always verify critical procedures against the current version of the applicable document.
