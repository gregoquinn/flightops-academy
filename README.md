# FlightOps Academy

### Professional operations training for Norfolk Island Airport and Australian certified aerodromes

---

FlightOps Academy is a structured study handbook for candidates preparing for **Flight Operations Officer (FOO)**, **Aerodrome Reporting Officer (ARO)**, and **Works Safety Officer (WSO)** roles under CASA Part 139.

All content is written directly against the regulatory framework — CASR Part 139, MOS Part 139, ICAO Annex 14, and the AVISS00053 ARO Skill Set — with specific focus on operations at **Norfolk Island Airport (YSNF)**.

## About Norfolk Island Airport (YSNF)

This handbook places particular emphasis on YSNF operations. Key characteristics:

- **Runway:** 11/29 — 1,951m × 45m, asphalt, code 4C
- **Elevation:** 371ft (113m) AMSL
- **PAPI:** 3.0° both runway directions
- **Time zone:** UTC+11:30 (NFST) / UTC+12:30 (NFDT) — does not follow AEDT
- **Primary hazard:** Sea fog — can close the aerodrome in under 5 minutes
- **Wildlife:** Masked Booby (high strike risk), Norfolk Island Green Parrot (EPBC Act protected)
- **Logistics:** Single runway, fuel by ship, regular medevac operations
